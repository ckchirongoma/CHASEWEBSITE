CCID: Research Operation Transformation

Urban Research 6 weeks implementation

Challenge

CCID's lean research team was responsible for collecting and maintaining city-wide business data across Cape Town. With limited resources, they needed to improve both data quality and processing speed while maintaining comprehensive coverage of their business database.

  • Manual data validation was time-consuming and error-prone
  • Inconsistent data entry across different team members
  • Reporting delays affecting decision-making timelines
  • Growing data volume overwhelming existing processes

Our Approach

We designed a 4-agent system that automated the entire research workflow from data collection to report generation:

Compliance Agent

Real-time QAQC & risk flagging system that validates data as it's entered and flags potential issues immediately.

Field Completion Agent

Automated data validation and enrichment that fills missing fields and standardizes entries.

Sweeper Agent

Inbox processing system that routes incoming data requests and updates to appropriate team members.

Storyteller Agent

Automated report generation that creates consistent, comprehensive reports from collected data.

Technology Stack

Airtable, Supabase, Firecrawl, Perplexity, Google Sheets, Composio, Pipedream

Impact & Results

75% Reduction in data error rates
5,000+ Business profiles enriched
24 hours New reporting SLA
60% Time saved on data processing
"Working with Charles and his team transformed our research operation. They redesigned our data pipeline, added automated QA and reporting agents, and dramatically improved data quality and turnaround time. The team now gets faster, reliable insight for decision-makers."
— Tasso Evangelinos, CEO, CCID
